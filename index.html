Requirements

NOT EVERYONE
MAKES THE LIST.

Six non-negotiable standards every barber must demonstrate. No exceptions. No shortcuts.

01
Consistency
Every client. Every time. Not just on a good day — the same level of precision every single appointment.
02
Precision
Sharp lines, clean blends, flawless transitions. Technical skill is the minimum — not the exception.
03
Customer Service
How you treat clients is as important as how you cut. Punctual, professional, personable every time.
04
Clean Environment
Your station, your tools, your shop. Cleanliness reflects professionalism. Non-negotiable.
05
Verified Portfolio
Your work must be documented and consistent. Recent content showing your real skill level — not your best day.
06
Reputation
What do your clients say? Referrals, reviews and repeat business tell us everything we need to know.
